Reimagined Card Deck: Studio Ghibli

For this project, I chose to create a deck of playing cards inspired by Studio Ghibli. Each suit represents a different movie, with characters and imagery incorporated throughout. The design choices were informed by competitor analysis and user feedback, resulting in a cohesive look that caters to both Ghibli fans and card game enthusiasts.
