Brand U

In this project, I dove deep into the essence of my personal brand, fusing my passion for computer science with a sprinkle of science fiction allure. Drawing inspiration from iconic scenes like those in the "Matrix" and classic coding visuals, I crafted a captivating 5-30-second video showcasing my brand logotype. With a palette of greens, blacks, and off-whites reminiscent of vintage computer screens, paired with a blend of old-school typing sounds and futuristic tones, the piece encapsulates the essence of coding and computer science. Through meticulous storyboarding and attention to detail in After Effects, I brought to life a dynamic narrative that speaks to both my technical expertise and creative flair.
